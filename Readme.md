### What I do

- **Backend** — Design and ship production APIs: authentication, payments and billing, real-time messaging, and the unglamorous reliability work that keeps them up (idempotency, retries, audit trails, observability).
- **AI automation** — Wire LLMs into real workflows: inbound lead ingestion, email and document parsing, classification, and human-in-the-loop pipelines orchestrated with n8n and webhooks.
- **Data** — PostgreSQL schema design, migrations, and read/write-split pipelines that feed both applications and models without stepping on each other.
